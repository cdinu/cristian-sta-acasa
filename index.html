Life. Universe. Everything*

On my birthday I used to get on the bike and cycle to somewhere and back for a couple of days. Not too far, not too close. A good opportunity to get some reflection, sport, rest. There was also the reason of my birthday being more a commemoration rather and less of a celebration, ever since I was about 21.

This year I keep the social distance. I stay at home.

We are all in a special situation, but I would like to enjoy ourselves together.

So I organize the first (which I know of) electronic birthday. Safe for all, accessible and free :)

Draft agenda (London time)

  • 13:30 - 15:30 - Not interesting, unless you speak Romanian 17:30 - 18:30 - English language section. Hellos, chats, some games

Some info and rules

  • I set up a Zoom meeting where we can hear and see each other. The room can accomodate 100 people, so there is plenty of (electronic) space.
  • Part of the event will be broadcast live online (YouTube, Twitch, maybe Facebook). If you are too shy to want to speak, you can use the chat there and watch the video stream.
  • Enter and leave as you wish
  • Be respectful to the others
  • Use real names
  • Please turn on your camera. All of us look good, even if we haven't had a haircut in a while.
